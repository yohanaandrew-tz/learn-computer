Introduction to Computer

The word Computer is derivered from the word Compute. Compute means to calculate. Computer originally was defined as a super calculator. It has the capacity to solve the complex arithmetic and scientific problems at very high speed. But nowadays in addition to handling complex arithmetic computations, Computer perferm many tasks like accepting, sorting, selecting and comparing various type of informations. They also perform arithmetic and logical operations on alphabetic, numeric and other type of informations

Therefore Computer is an electronic device, oparating under the control of instructions stored in instructions in its own memory, that can accept data(input), process them according to specified rules, produce(output) and store the results(storage) for future use.

Introduction to Microsoft office

Microsoft office is aset of Computer applications mainly used for business or office purposes. First introduced in 1990, Office software is made by Microsoft Corporation.

The most common Office applications are:
  • Microsoft Word: It is used to make and edit documents such as letters, reports, resumes and run spell-checks on writting.
  • Microsoft Excel: is an electronic spreedsheet program that helps you to store, organize and manipulate data by Creating simple to complex spreedsheet.
  • Microsoft PowerPoint: allows you to visually display information, using anything from basic slideshow to professional multimidia presentations.
  • Microsoft Outlook: is a personal information manager mainly used for emails but that can be also used to store calendars and contacts information, manage tasks as well as organize meetings.
  • Microsoft Publisher: is the graphic design app that gives users creating materials for marketing or publications more options in the layout and design of their documents.
  • Microsoft Access: is the Database Management System that allows you to link and use data from other sources, manipulate the data you have collected in different ways, as well as create simple bussines applications.
  • Microsoft OneNote: is a digital alternative to a paper notebook that allows you to create , organize and share your notes easily.
